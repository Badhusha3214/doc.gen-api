Event Description The "hello" event was an introductory programming workshop aimed at teaching students the fundamentals of coding. Participants were introduced to the concept of "Hello World," a classic first program in programming. The workshop focused on understanding basic syntax, variables, and the process of running a simple program.
Feedback The feedback received from participants was positive, indicating a successful and engaging learning experience.
Program Outcome The event successfully introduced students to the basic concepts of programming. Participants were able to understand the structure of a basic program and comprehend the significance of the "Hello World" program as a starting point in the programming journey.
