Event Description
    The "Hello World" workshop aimed to introduce students to the fundamentals of programming by guiding them through the creation of their first program, the classic "Hello World" output. The workshop provided a hands-on learning experience, covering basic syntax, variable declaration, and input/output operations.
  • *Program Outcome:** Through engaging exercises and expert guidance, all participants successfully completed the "Hello World" program, gaining a foundational understanding of programming concepts and a sense of accomplishment in their first coding endeavor.
  • *Feedback:** The workshop received positive feedback, with participants praising the clear explanations, interactive format, and supportive environment.
